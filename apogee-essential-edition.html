Apogee Essential Edition brings the reliability and automation of the Apogee prepress platform to small commercial printing operations. It is purposefully scoped to match the production volumes and budgets of shops processing up to 2,500 m² of plates per year — removing the complexity and cost overhead that comes with larger-scale systems.

Whether you are handling brochures, flyers, business stationery, or short-run commercial jobs, Apogee Essential Edition gives your team a single, streamlined environment to manage every step from incoming PDF to press-ready plate output.

Key Features

Everything a small print shop needs — nothing it does not.

📄

PDF Preflight & Normalization

Automatically checks incoming PDF files against configurable rules before they enter production. Catches errors early, reducing costly reruns.

📋

Automated Imposition

Calculates optimal sheet layouts for your press format automatically, minimizing paper waste and manual layout time on every job.

⚙

Hot Folder Automation

Drop files into watched folders and let the workflow handle normalization, imposition, and output automatically — no operator intervention required for standard jobs.

🌈

Color Management

Built-in ICC-based color management ensures consistent, predictable output across different substrates and output devices.

📷

Plate-Ready Output

Generates high-quality 1-bit TIFF files optimized for your CtP device, supporting the full range of ECO3 platesetters and plates.

🔗

Apogee Architecture

Built on the same proven modular platform as the full Apogee system, ensuring stability, long-term support, and a clear upgrade path as your business grows.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Apogee Essential Edition?
Apogee Essential Edition is a prepress workflow software designed for small commercial print shops with fewer than 10 employees and a plate volume up to 2,500 m² per year. It is based on the proven Apogee architecture and includes PDF preflight, automated imposition, and plate-ready output.
Who is Apogee Essential Edition designed for?
The product targets small to medium commercial printers with up to 10 employees and an annual plate volume of approximately 2,500 m². It delivers core Apogee workflow capabilities at the right scale and cost for smaller operations.
How is Apogee Essential Edition different from the full Apogee?
Apogee Essential Edition is built on the same Apogee architecture but is optimized for lower processing volumes, making it more affordable and simpler to operate. Advanced modules such as MIS/JDF integration, packaging support, and WebApproval are not included but are available in the full Apogee product.
Can I upgrade from Apogee Essential to the full Apogee later?
Yes. Because both products share the same Apogee platform, upgrading to the full Apogee edition does not require replacing your infrastructure or retraining your team. Contact ECO3 or your local reseller to discuss an upgrade path.
How is Apogee Essential Edition licensed?
Apogee Essential Edition is available under a commercial software license. Contact ECO3 or your local ECO3 reseller for pricing and licensing details specific to your region.
What ECO3 platesetters and plates are compatible?
Apogee Essential Edition is compatible with the full range of ECO3 Avalon CtP platesetters and ECO3 offset plates, including Azura, Energy Elite, and Avatar plate families. Consult your ECO3 representative to confirm compatibility with your specific hardware configuration.
